OmniHacks

September 21 - 22, 2019 @Holberton SF, CA

REGISTER TODAY

This September, 200 high school students from the Bay Area will gather at OmniHacks to conceive innovative products and realize their potential in solving some of the biggest societal problems.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24 hour long event where students work together to create a project from scratch. Get ready to learn a lot while having fun!

What if I donâ€™t know how to code?

There will be workshops for all skill levels, and we encourage you to partake in these workshops to get a jumpstart before you start to develop your project.

What can my team build?

Anything realistic that you can imagine! Remember, there will be a theme and different categories for prizes. Projects will be judged on their Impact, Usability, Creativity, and Complexity.

What should I bring?

A valid school ID, signed waivers, a laptop, charger(s), and toiletries. Remember to bring your hardware as well.

Who can attend OmniHacks?

All high school students are welcome! No prior experience is necessary.

How much does it cost to attend?

Thanks to our sponsors, OmniHacks absolutely FREE!

How do I get there?

Holberton is 5 minutes away from the Powell Street BART station
