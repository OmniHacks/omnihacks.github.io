Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24 hour long event where students work together to create a project from scratch. Get ready to learn a lot!

What if I donâ€™t know how to code?

OmniHacks has workshops catering to all levels of hackers and we encourage you to partake in these workshops to get a jumpstart before you start to develop your project.

What can my team build?

Any tech product. Hardware, Software or even a combination of both. Remember, that there will be a theme and different categories for prizes. Projects will be judged on their Impact, Usability, Creativity, and Complexity.

What should I bring?

A valid school ID, signed waivers, a laptop, charger(s), and toiletries. For those planning to create a hardware project, please bring whatever parts you may need.

How much does it cost to attend?

Thanks to our sponsors, OmniHacks absolutely FREE!

Who can attend OmniHacks?

All high school students are welcome! No prior experience is necessary.

How do I get there?

Holberton SF is 5 minutes away from the Powell Street BART station
